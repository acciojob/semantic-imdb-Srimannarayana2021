Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from within people's subconscious through shared dreams. He is offered a chance to erase his criminal past if he can successfully implant an idea into a target's mind, a process known as "inception." However, as the mission unfolds, Cobb faces challenges from his own past that threaten the success of the operation.
